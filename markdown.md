# GemmaForge: The Aletheia Protocol

GemmaForge is a high-performance intelligence orchestration engine powered by **Gemma 4**. It is designed to transform raw technical seeds into multi-platform distribution payloads.

## 🚀 The Distribution Mesh (Pipeline)
The main pipeline is an automated "God-Loop" that executes the following sequence:
1.  **SERP Research**: Fetches live organic search results for your topic.
2.  **Gap Analysis**: Identifies what competitors missed compared to your unique seed.
3.  **Blueprint Architecting**: The 26B MoE model designs a 5-section technical structure.
4.  **Production Writing**: The 31B Dense model generates the final technical HTML.
5.  **Syndication**: Creates Markdown summaries for social distribution.

---

## 🧩 Atomic Tools (Modular Suite)
Use these tools to execute specific AI tasks independently via the "Atomic Tools" tab.

### 1. Analyze Trends
- **Function**: Processes raw search or trend data.
- **Outcome**: Identifies the top 3-5 rising technical search terms for content planning.

### 2. Plan Content (Blueprint)
- **Function**: Acts as a "Lead Architect" to design the technical structure of a post.
- **Outcome**: A detailed "Meta-Prompt" or blueprint that guides a writing model.

### 3. Generate HTML
- **Function**: The "Principal Engineer" phase of the pipeline.
- **Outcome**: Pure, semantic HTML with accurate code examples and engineering depth.

### 4. Generate Markdown
- **Function**: Syndication logic that converts long-form technical content into social summaries.
- **Outcome**: A punchy, 300-word engineering summary optimized for social platforms.

### 5. Extract Tech Signal
- **Function**: A "De-Fluffer" that processes competitor content or research papers.
- **Outcome**: Removes marketing jargon and business filler, leaving only raw technical facts.

---

## 🛡️ Aletheia Mode
Toggle this on to run the entire intelligence pipeline safely. It performs all AI reasoning and content generation but **skips live broadcasting and quality audits**, allowing you to validate the "Seed" before going live.
